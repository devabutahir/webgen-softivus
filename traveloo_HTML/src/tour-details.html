The Appalachian Trail, spanning over 2,190 miles from Georgia to Maine, is one of the most iconic long-distance hiking trails in the world. This breathtaking path traverses 14 states, offering hikers an unparalleled opportunity to experience the diverse beauty of the eastern United States. From the dense forests of the Smoky Mountains to the rugged peaks of New Hampshire's White Mountains, the trail presents a variety of landscapes and ecosystems. Whether you're a seasoned thru-hiker tackling the entire trail or a day hiker exploring a section, the Appalachian Trail promises adventure and tranquility. Along the way, you'll encounter charming small towns, historic sites, and abundant wildlife
