Bintan, Indonesia

Bintan, a tropical paradise located just a short ferry ride from Singapore, offers pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and a blend of vibrant culture and serene luxury. Known for its crystal-clear waters and powdery white sands, Bintan is the perfect getaway for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike. Explore the rich cultural heritage at Senggarang and Penyengat Island, or dive into thrilling water sports such as snorkeling, diving, and jet-skiing. For those seeking relaxation, Bintan's world-class resorts provide an idyllic escape with luxurious amenities and stunning ocean views. Indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment, savor fresh seafood at local eateries, or tee off at one of the island’s renowned golf courses.

The underwater world around Bintan is teeming with vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life, making it a haven for divers and snorkelers. For those who prefer staying on land, the island offers world-class golf courses with breathtaking ocean views and challenging fairways.

For relaxation, Bintan's luxury resorts provide an idyllic escape with top-notch amenities, private beaches, and rejuvenating spa treatments. Indulge in fresh, locally-sourced seafood at beachfront restaurants or enjoy a romantic sunset dinner with your toes in the sand. Nature enthusiasts can explore the island’s lush interior through mangrove tours, where you can spot unique wildlife and learn about the delicate ecosystems.
